NyaayaMind – AI-Powered Legal Intelligence System
⚖️ Making Legal Knowledge Accessible through AI

NyaayaMind is an AI-driven legal assistant that simplifies complex legal information into understandable insights using Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. It enables users to quickly interpret legal queries, making legal awareness more accessible and efficient.

🚀 Key Highlights

✨ AI-based legal query understanding
✨ Smart summarization of legal content
✨ Clean and interactive user interface
✨ Scalable and real-world applicable system
✨ Built with focus on accessibility and usability

🧩 Problem Statement

Legal information is often complex, lengthy, and difficult for common people to interpret. Access to quick and reliable legal guidance is limited.

➡️ NyaayaMind solves this by acting as an intelligent assistant that simplifies legal understanding in seconds.

💡 Solution Approach

The system leverages Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to process user queries and generate meaningful, simplified responses.

Workflow:
User inputs a legal query
NLP model processes and interprets intent
Relevant information is retrieved/generated
Output is displayed in a simplified format
